Quick Questions

Everything you'd ask before moving in.

Is this PG for men only?

Yes — Green Elite PG is exclusively for men. We maintain a respectful, professional environment focused on working men and students.

What is the security deposit?

We charge a refundable deposit equal to 1 month's tariff, returned within 7 days of check-out after inspection.

Are meals included in the tariff?

Yes — freshly prepared South Indian meals are included. RO water available 24×7 on every floor.

What is the minimum stay period?

We welcome both long and short-term stays. Please mention your tentative stay in the enquiry form — or skip it if you're unsure.

Do you offer individual lockers?

Yes. Every resident gets an individual locker for valuables, along with access to a shared fridge and washing machine.

Is there a curfew or gate timing?

Main gate closes at 11:00 PM. Late entry is allowed with prior intimation — we understand IT/manufacturing shift schedules.
